Brick Bounce

A simple challenge. Try your luck and block as many circles as you can!

Score: 0

High Score: 0

Instructions

  1. Click where you want to place the bricks. You only have 3 so choose wisely!
  2. Press the start button to create circles. They'll bounce if they hit a brick.
  3. The counter will keep track of how many circles you block and your high score.
  4. You can choose the color scheme for the circles too :)
  5. Press the clear button to remove your bricks and try again.
  6. Good luck!
